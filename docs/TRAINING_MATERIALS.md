## Training Delivery

### Online Learning
- **Platform**: Learning Management System (LMS)
- **Format**: Video, interactive modules, quizzes
- **Access**: 24/7 availability
- **Tracking**: Completion tracking, certificates

### Instructor-Led Training
- **Format**: Classroom, virtual classroom
- **Size**: Maximum 15 participants
- **Materials**: Presentation, handouts, exercises
- **Assessment**: Skills demonstration

### Hands-On Labs
- **Environment**: Training sandbox
- **Data**: De-identified test data
- **Scenarios**: Real-world workflows
- **Support**: Instructor assistance

## Assessment and Certification

### Knowledge Assessment
- **Format**: Multiple choice, true/false
- **Passing Score**: 80%
- **Attempts**: 3 attempts allowed
- **Certificate**: Issued upon passing

### Skills Assessment
- **Format**: Hands-on demonstration
- **Evaluation**: Checklist-based
- **Passing**: All critical tasks completed
- **Remediation**: Additional training if needed

### Certification Tracking
| Certification | Validity | Renewal |
|---------------|----------|---------|
| System User | 1 year | Annual refresher |
| Clinical User | 1 year | Annual refresher |
| Super User | 2 years | Bi-annual |
| Administrator | 2 years | Bi-annual |
| Trainer | 3 years | Tri-annual |

## Purpose

This matrix tracks ONC Health IT Certification Program requirements. **ONC certification requires formal testing by an authorized testing laboratory (ONC-ATL) and certification by an authorized certification body (ONC-ACB). This document tracks preparation status only.**

## Maintenance Requirements

After certification:

1. Annual surveillance by ONC-ACB
2. Corrective action for identified issues
3. Update certification for major changes
4. Maintain quality management system
5. Report adverse events
6. Maintain transparency disclosures
7. Participate in ONC oversight activities

## Certification Statement

**This product is NOT ONC certified.** ONC certification requires:

1. Complete implementation of all selected criteria
2. Successful testing by an authorized ONC-ATL
3. Certification by an authorized ONC-ACB
4. Ongoing compliance with certification requirements

No self-declaration or internal testing satisfies ONC certification requirements.
